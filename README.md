# TimeSeriesAnalytics-
This project explores the application of time 
series analysis techniques, specifically ARIMA 
(Autoregressive Integrated Moving Average) and LSTM 
(Long Short-Term Memory), for predicting sales data. 
Accurate sales forecasting is essential for effective 
inventory management and strategic planning across 
various industries. By analyzing historical sales patterns, 
the research identifies underlying trends and variations. 
The ARIMA and LSTM models are then implemented to 
forecast future sales, with a focus on evaluating their 
performance in terms of accuracy and reliability. This study 
contributes valuable insights into the effectiveness of time 
series methods in enhancing sales forecasting accuracy, 
providing a framework for businesses seeking to optimize 
inventory management and improve decision
making processes.
